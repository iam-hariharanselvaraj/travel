WHY DO WE TRAVEL IN MONSOON??

Nature comes to life, and forests and rivers swell with activity. Many tourists avoid travelling to India in the monsoon, which means you get the advantage of off-season costs. You also get to enjoy a lot of sights to yourself. That makes it easier to connect with the locals and experience more authenticity.
